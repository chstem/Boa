Frequently Asked Questions

Q: After editing the abstract or author information, the preview pdf will not be updated.
A: Try clearing your browser cache.

Q: What file types are supported for the figure?
A: Please upload your figure in one of the following formats: .jpeg (or .jpg), .png, .pdf.

Q: Can I upload more than one figure?
A: No, when uploading a new figure, the last one will be replaced. If you need two or more figures in your abstract, you can combine them in a single file before uploading.
